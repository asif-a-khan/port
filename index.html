Grant My Wish Zoltar

React | Javascript ES6 | HTML5/CSS3

A fun little app that fetches data from the 'Advice Slip' - API and pays omage to Zoltar from the movie 'Big' - 1988. The app allows users to make a wish to Zoltar by inserting a coin into his machine. The coin must then be navigated through a maze by the user. Upon completion, Zoltar will grant the user their wish and provide advice to them.

Created by: Asif Khan, Barrie Wu, Prachie Gujar & Tenal Bourchier
